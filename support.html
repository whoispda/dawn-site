Common questions

How do I get started?

Open Dawn and start logging — there's no account or sign-up. Tap a card for a feed, sleep, or diaper. After a few entries, Dawn begins learning your baby's rhythm.

Where is my data, and is it safe?

Everything stays on your device and, if you use iCloud, in your own private iCloud account. Dawn has no server and collects nothing. See our Privacy Policy.

Can I use Dawn on more than one device?

Yes. With iCloud turned on, your data syncs across your own iPhone and iPad automatically, so it's always up to date and backed up.

How do I export a summary for my pediatrician?

Open Reports and choose Export — Dawn builds a tidy PDF of the week or month you pick that you can print or email.

Something's not working / I have an idea.

Please email hello@kairofasting.com. Bug reports and feature ideas genuinely shape what we build next.

Is Dawn medical advice?

No. Dawn records and visualizes what you log and shows patterns from your own data. It is not a diagnostic tool — always check with your pediatrician.
