Good to know

Questions parents ask

Is Dawn free?

Dawn is free to use, including the on-device predictions other apps put behind a subscription. A future optional upgrade may add extras, but the core tracking and forecasts stay free.

Where does my data go?

Onto your device, and — if you use iCloud — into your own private iCloud account for backup and sync across your devices. It never reaches us. We have no server and collect nothing.

Does it work for two parents?

Yes — your Dawn data syncs across your own devices through private iCloud, so your phones stay in step. We're exploring shared access between two parents' separate accounts.

Is this medical advice?

No. Dawn records and visualizes what you log and shows patterns from your own data. It isn't a diagnostic tool — always check with your pediatrician.
